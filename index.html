I am passionate about leveraging mathematical optimization approaches to solve real-world problems in machine learning.

Research Interests

I'm conducting research to answer the question: What makes a good optimizer?

  • Mathematical and Stochastic Optimization in machine learning (e.g., Online learning)
  • Utilizing (mathematical) theory to solve real-world problems
  • Developing novel optimizers that achieve good performance faster and memory/computationally efficient.

I'm also very interested in methodologies for training AI with energy efficiency in mind, especially given the significant power consumption of AI data centers and GPU servers.
